Our Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred nutrition consulting service available to cattle and sheep producers in Western Canada. We establish strong relationships with individual operations to develop effective and sustainable nutrition programs that are specific to their operation. We manufacture mineral specific for your operation.

Our Misson

Our passion is the cattle industry.  We work with innovative producers to fine tune their operations to make them more productive, sustainable and profitable. Our professional and experienced nutritionists can help you build a robust and feasible nutrition program that will work for you. If there is one thing that is constant with farming and ranching, it's change. We are there to help you adapt your program to weather challenges, feed availability and market conditions.

Our History

Blue Rock Minerals was established in 1998 by Lee Eddy to serve cattle producers across Western Canada. In 2013, Lee partnered with Kristen Ritson-Bennett to purchase a premix manufacturing facility in Central Alberta. Today, Blue Rock is made up of a team of independent ruminant nutrition consultants who support producers with customized solutions. As part of servicing clients, the company operates a premix manufacturing facility that produces custom mineral premixes designed specifically for each operation, working with cow-calf, backgrounding, feedlot, bison, and small ruminant producers.
